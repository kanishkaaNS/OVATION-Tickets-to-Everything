Help for booking and checkout.

Find quick answers for selecting a city, adding tickets to your cart, completing checkout, and viewing order confirmation.

Booking tickets

Choose your city, open an event, select ticket quantities, and use Add to cart or Checkout to continue.

Cart and checkout

You can update quantities, remove tickets, and complete the demo payment form to generate an order confirmation.

Changing cities

Use the city selector in the header to refresh event listings for another popular city.
